Frequently Asked Questions

How does the adoption process work?

Our adoption process is designed to be simple and straightforward. Browse our listings, find a pet you love, and click the "Adopt" button. You'll be guided through the necessary steps, including filling out an application and communicating with the shelter or rescuer.

What are the adoption fees?

Adoption fees vary depending on the animal, its age, and the rescue organization. The fee is listed on each pet's profile and typically covers vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and other initial veterinary care.

Can I meet a pet before adopting?

Yes, we encourage it! Most shelters and rescues are happy to arrange a meet-and-greet. Please contact the organization directly through the information provided on the pet's profile to schedule a visit.
